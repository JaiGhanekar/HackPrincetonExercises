p.teach

(physical teach)

lifting incorrectly can be dangerous.

you know when you're just trying to exercise but then you make one wrong move and suddenly you're just laying there seriously injured and you're not sure what went wrong? p.teach can fix that.

about

p.teach is a tool made with the myo armband. the myo armband offers wireless gesture and motion control. using analytics and metrics collected from the myo user, p.teach acts as your personal trainer to tell you whether you are performing various exercises correctly.

how p.teach works

when you exercise, just put the myo on your arm and connect to the server. p.teach will encourage you when your lifting technique is correct, and alert you with a short vibration when it is incorrect so that you can fix your form.
